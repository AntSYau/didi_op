## Local CNN - First Law of Geography (Tobler, 1970) - “near things are more related than distant things”. - Generate $S\times S$ image $\mathrm{Y}_t^i\in \mathbb{R}^{S\times S\times1}$ for each location $i$ at each time $t$. - $\mathrm{Y}_t^{i,k}=f(\mathrm{Y}_t^{i-1}*\mathrm{W}_t^k+\textrm{b}_t^k)$
